Some thoughts on software development.

My name is Ryan Sadler and I’m a Staff Software Engineer at Reddit. You can find me on LinkedIn.

I’ve worked as a software developer for over 20 years now. Coding was always a hobby of mine. My first computer was a Commodore Vic20, with 3KB of RAM. It was simple enough I could figure it out how it worked at the hardware level. The manual that came with the computer was complete with schematics.

These days I like to code in Rust and Python. My latest project is a hotreloadable ELF linker. More details will hopefully find their way onto the blog.
